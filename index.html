Yes.
How many benchmarks can I compare at the same time?
14
How do I export an image of everything?
Press the camera button in the top-right.
How do I add comments to the benchmarks?
Press the pencil button next to the file name to edit the benchmark's comment.
To add persistent comments add //Comment= and your comment to the first line of the CSV.
How do I display the frame time graph in milliseconds instead of FPS?
Press the upper-right FPS / ms toggle.
How do I crop data sets in the individual benchmark area?
Use the number input fields below the benchmarks or hold ALT and scroll inside the frame time graph.
How do I crop data sets in the comparison area?
Hold ALT and scroll inside the frame time graph.
How do the multi-select fields in the comparison area work?
CTRL-click to select / deselect options.
